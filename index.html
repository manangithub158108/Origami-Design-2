A camel is an even-toed ungulate in the genus Camelus that bears distinctive fatty deposits known as "humps" on its back.


Pigeon is a French word that derives from the Latin pipio, for a "peeping" chick, while dove is a Germanic word that refers to the bird's diving flight.


The giant panda also known as the panda bear or simply the panda, is a bear native to south central China. It is characterised by large, black patches around its eyes, over the ears, and across its round body.


Chameleons are distinguished by their zygodactylous feet; their very extensive, not highly modified, rapidly extrudable tongues; their swaying gait; and crests or horns on their brow and snout.
